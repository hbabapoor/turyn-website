What We Build

Five Areas of Focused Expertise

Our capabilities span the full spectrum of bespoke residential construction across metropolitan Sydney — from intimate family homes to complex dual-occupancy and joint-venture developments.

01

Freestanding Houses

Custom single dwellings built to your specifications — from striking contemporary architectural homes to classic family residences. Every build is entirely unique.

02

Knockdown Rebuilds

End-to-end demolition and rebuild on your existing lot, including service disconnections and full utility re-establishment. Stay in the street you love.

03

Duplex Developments

A core strength of the business. Side-by-side and attached duplex projects with dual-occupancy approvals and Torrens-title subdivisions across Sydney.

04

Extensions & Renovations

Ground-floor and first-floor additions, full home renovations, and structural alterations — delivered with precision and minimal disruption to your life.

05

Subdivisions

Lower-density residential subdivision works with civil works, service provisioning, and dwelling construction on subdivided lots across metropolitan Sydney.

06

Investor Joint Ventures

TURYN acts as buyer's agent, licensed builder, and developer partner for private investors — combining construction expertise with capital to unlock value across Sydney's residential market.

How We Work

A Process Built on Clarity

From first consultation to handover, every TURYN project is personally managed by our directors. No confusion, no surprises — just a seamless journey to your completed home.

01

Initial Consultation

We begin with a genuine conversation — listening to your vision, priorities, lifestyle, and budget. Both directors are present from the first meeting. This is where the relationship and brief are formed.

02

Design & Architecture

Your brief is translated into a bespoke architectural concept. You'll be involved at every stage — from floor plan to facade — refining until the design is exactly right for your land and lifestyle.

03

Contract & Approvals

We handle all town planning, certification, and approvals coordination. Our fixed-price contracts are transparent and all-inclusive — no surprises or hidden costs at any stage.

04

Construction

Both directors are personally on-site throughout the build. We work professionally with lenders and quantity surveyors, provide regular progress updates, and maintain disciplined cost and schedule control.

05

Handover & Beyond

We walk you personally through your completed home. Our relationship with clients does not end at the front door — we remain available for any post-handover questions and ongoing support.
