Our Story

Built on Technical Depth & Hands-On Delivery

TURYN Pty Ltd was established in 2015 as a Sydney-based residential building company. Although the company itself is ten years young, it is led by two directors who are both licensed builders and qualified civil engineers, with extensive prior experience across building, engineering, and construction management.

Our portfolio covers freestanding homes, knockdown rebuilds, duplex developments, extensions and renovations, and lower-density residential subdivisions across metropolitan Sydney. We have a particular reputation for duplex projects and high-quality renovation works, and act as a trusted joint-venture partner for private investors.

Our approach is built on technical depth, hands-on involvement, and a minimal record of client complaints across every project we deliver.

Freestanding Houses
